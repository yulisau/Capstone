Graphic Toggle

The main screen features moving visuals that help relax users as they watch. The rythmic repetitiveness of the graphic helps calm the mind.

Play around with the toggles at the bottom of the graphic to make more appear/disappear.

Audio Toggle

The right drop down menu features four different audios available for toggling. Each toggle changes an aspect of the audio for a more personalized experience.

Try layering different sounds to find what works for you.
